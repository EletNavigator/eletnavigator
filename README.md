# ÉletNavigátor

**ÉletNavigátor** is a simple and privacy-focused web application designed to support self-reflection, conscious living, and personal growth.
In a digital world full of notifications, advertisements, and constant distractions, ÉletNavigátor aims to create a calmer space where people can slow down, reflect, and focus on themselves.

## 🌱 Project Story
ÉletNavigátor was created from a personal idea: a digital space where people do not need to leave technology behind in order to find a moment of calm.
I am the creator behind the project. My goal was creating a simple, meaningful and human-centered web experience.
The project is developed independently through continuous learning and improvement.

## 🔒 Principles
ÉletNavigátor is built around simplicity and privacy:

- No advertisements
- No tracking
- No unnecessary data collection
- No intrusive cookies
- Simple and lightweight design

The goal is to create a digital space that respects users and their attention.
